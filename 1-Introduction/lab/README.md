# Lab: From Data to Decision

---

## Objective

In this lab, you will learn how to:

* Explore raw data
* Transform it into information
* Derive insights
* Make a decision

---

## Key Idea

> Data is only valuable when it leads to action.

---

## Scenario

You are given student engagement data:

* Number of logins
* Assignment submission status
* Forum participation

Your task is to determine:

> Which students may require intervention.

---

## Tools

You can choose to complete this lab using:

* Python (`python/`)
* R (`r/`)

---

## Tasks

1. Explore the dataset
2. Summarise key patterns
3. Identify at-risk students
4. Recommend an action

---

## Deliverable

Write a short response:

* What did you observe?
* What does it mean?
* What should be done?

---

## Reminder

> The goal is not to produce output.
> The goal is to **make a decision based on data**.
