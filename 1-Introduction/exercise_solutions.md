# Exercise Solutions: Thinking from Data to Decision

---

## Scenario 1

* Logged in 2 times
* Did not submit assignment
* No forum activity

### 1. From Data to Information

* Low login frequency
* Assignment not submitted
* No participation in forum

---

### 2. From Information to Insight

* The student shows low engagement
* There may be a risk of falling behind or dropping out

---

### 3. From Insight to Decision

* Follow up with the student
* Send a reminder or reach out for support
* Monitor the student more closely

---

## Scenario 2

* Logged in 8 times
* Submitted assignment
* Active in forum

### Interpretation

* The student is actively engaged
* No immediate intervention is required

---

## Key Reflection

* Data becomes useful when it is interpreted in context
* Insights are actionable when they inform decisions
