# Exercise: Thinking from Data to Decision

---

## Objective

Practice translating data into information, insight, and decision.

---

## Scenario

You are given the following information about a student:

* Logged in 2 times this week
* Did not submit assignment
* No forum activity

---

## Questions

### 1. From Data to Information

What information can you derive?

---

### 2. From Information to Insight

What does this suggest about the student?

---

### 3. From Insight to Decision

What action would you recommend?

---

## Extension

Another student:

* Logged in 8 times
* Submitted assignment
* Active in forum

What is your conclusion?

---

## How this connects

In this exercise, you are interpreting a single student scenario.

In the lab, you will apply the same thinking to a full dataset.

---

## Reflection

* When does data become useful?
* What makes an insight actionable?

---

## Reminder

The goal is not to analyse data using tools.

The goal is to think clearly about what the data means and what should be done.
