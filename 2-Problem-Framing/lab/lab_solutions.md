# Lab Solutions: Problem Framing (Example)

---

## Example Response

### Business Objective

Improve student retention by identifying disengaged students early.

---

### Decision

Determine which students require intervention in the first few weeks.

---

### Success Metrics

* Reduction in dropout rate
* Increase in assignment completion
* Increase in engagement levels

---

### Analytical Question

Which students show early signs of disengagement based on login, submission, and participation behaviour?

---

### Assumptions

* Engagement is a proxy for academic risk
* Early intervention improves outcomes

---

### Constraints

* Limited access to real-time data
* Definitions of engagement may vary

---

## Note

There is no single correct answer.

A good solution is one that:

* is clear
* is actionable
* links data to decision
