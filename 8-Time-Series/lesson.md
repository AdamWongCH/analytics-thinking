# Lesson: Time Series

---

## Objective

Understand how data behaves over time.

---

## The Common Mistake

Many learners treat time series like normal data.

But time adds:

* sequence
* trends
* patterns

---

## What is Time Series?

Time series data is:

* ordered by time
* sequential
* dependent on previous values

---

## Key Patterns

### Trend

Long-term increase or decrease

---

### Seasonality

Repeated patterns over time

---

### Noise

Random fluctuations

---

## Example

Student logins over weeks:

* increasing trend → improving engagement
* sudden drop → possible issue

---

## Why This Matters

Time-based patterns help:

* detect problems early
* monitor behaviour
* guide interventions

---

## How this connects

In the exercise, you will:

* identify patterns

In the lab, you will:

* visualise and interpret time series

---

## Key Takeaway

Time adds meaning to data patterns.
