# Exercise Solutions: Time Series

---

## Trend

Look for:

* upward movement → increasing engagement
* downward movement → declining engagement

---

## Patterns

Repeated spikes or dips may indicate:

* weekly cycles
* assessment periods

---

## Changes

Sudden changes may indicate:

* system issues
* deadlines
* behavioural shifts

---

## Note

Time helps us detect changes that static data cannot show.
