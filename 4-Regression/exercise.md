# Exercise: Interpreting Relationships

---

## Objective

Practice interpreting relationships between variables.

---

## Scenario

You are given the following information:

* Students with more logins tend to score higher
* Students with fewer logins tend to score lower

---

## Tasks

### 1. Interpret the Relationship

What does this suggest?

---

### 2. Think About Causes

Does this mean logins cause better performance?

---

### 3. Identify Limitations

What other factors might influence performance?

---

## How this connects

In this exercise, you interpret relationships.

In the lab, you will build a regression model.

---

## Reflection

* Why is correlation not the same as causation?
