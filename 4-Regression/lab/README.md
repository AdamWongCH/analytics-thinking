# Lab: Regression

---

## Objective

Build and interpret a simple regression model to understand relationships between variables.

---

## How this lab connects

In the lesson, you learned what regression is.

In the exercise, you interpreted relationships.

In this lab, you will build a model, visualise relationships, and interpret the results.

---

## Scenario

You are given student engagement data and performance scores.

Your task is to understand how engagement relates to performance.

---

## Tasks

### Step 1: Explore the Data

* Understand the variables
* Identify input and target variables

---

### Step 2: Summarise the Data

* Generate summary statistics
* Identify variation in the data

---

### Step 3: Visualise Relationships

* Plot engagement variables against performance
* Look for patterns in the data

---

### Step 4: Build the Model

* Use engagement variables to predict performance

---

### Step 5: Interpret Results

* What do the coefficients suggest?
* Are the relationships meaningful?

---

### Step 6: Evaluate the Model

* How well does the model fit the data?
* Is the prediction error acceptable?

---

### Step 7: Reflect on Usefulness

* Is this model useful for decision-making?
* What are its limitations?

---

## Deliverable

Write a short summary covering:

* Relationship between variables
* Model interpretation
* Evaluation of performance
* Limitations
* Decision usefulness
