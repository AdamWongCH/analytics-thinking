# Exercise: Prioritising Decisions

---

## Objective

Decide which students to prioritise.

---

## Scenario

You are given 10 students with risk scores.

You can only intervene on 3 students.

---

## Data

| Student | Risk Score |
| ------- | ---------- |
| A       | 0.90       |
| B       | 0.85       |
| C       | 0.80       |
| D       | 0.70       |
| E       | 0.60       |
| F       | 0.55       |
| G       | 0.50       |
| H       | 0.45       |
| I       | 0.40       |
| J       | 0.30       |

---

## Tasks

1. Select 3 students to prioritise
2. Explain your choice

---

## Reflection

* Why did you choose these students?
* Would your decision change if resources increase?
