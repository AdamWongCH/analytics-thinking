# Exercise: Understanding Networks

---

## Objective

Interpret relationships and structure in a simple network.

---

## Scenario

You are given a small interaction network between students.

Each connection represents a student interacting with another student.

---

## Tasks

1. Identify the most connected student
2. Identify any isolated or less connected students
3. Identify possible groups or clusters of students
4. Explain what these patterns suggest about student interaction

---

## Reflection

* Why might some students be more connected than others?
* What might isolation indicate?
* What role might highly connected students play in the network?
* How could this information support decision-making?
