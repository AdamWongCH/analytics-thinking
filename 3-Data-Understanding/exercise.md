# Exercise: Understanding the Data

---

## Objective

Practice interpreting data before analysis.

---

## Scenario

You are given the following data:

| student | logins | submitted | forum |
| ------- | ------ | --------- | ----- |
| A       | 10     | Yes       | 5     |
| B       | 1      | No        | 0     |
| C       | 5      | Yes       | 2     |

---

## Tasks

### 1. Identify Patterns

What differences do you observe?

---

### 2. Interpret the Data

What might the data suggest about each student?

---

### 3. Identify Issues

What limitations or missing context do you see?

---

## How this connects

In this exercise, you interpret small data.

In the lab, you will explore a dataset using tools.
