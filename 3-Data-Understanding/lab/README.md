# Lab: Data Understanding

---

## Objective

Explore a dataset and understand its structure, patterns, and quality.

---

## How this lab connects

In the lesson, you learned how to understand data.

In the exercise, you interpreted small examples.

In this lab, you will explore a dataset using tools.

---

## Scenario

You are given a dataset of student engagement.

---

## Tasks

### Step 1: Explore the Data

* View the dataset
* Understand the columns

---

### Step 2: Summarise the Data

* Generate summary statistics
* Identify patterns

---

### Step 3: Check Data Quality

* Look for missing values
* Identify unusual values

---

### Step 4: Initial Insights

* What patterns do you observe?
* Which students may be at risk?

---

## Deliverable

Write a short summary:

* Key patterns
* Data issues
* Initial insights

---

## Reminder

Understanding data comes before modelling.
