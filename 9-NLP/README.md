---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

* understand what NLP is
* work with text data
* identify common words and themes
* perform simple sentiment analysis
* interpret textual data meaningfully

---

## Why This Module Matters

Many important insights are hidden in text.

For example:

* student feedback
* survey responses
* emails and messages

Structured data tells us *what* happened.

Text helps us understand:

> **why it happened**

---

## The Core Idea

NLP helps us extract meaning from text.

It allows us to turn unstructured text into usable insights.

---

## Scenario

You are given student feedback comments.

Your task is to:

* identify common themes
* analyse sentiment
* highlight possible issues
