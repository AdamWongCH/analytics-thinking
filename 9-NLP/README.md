# 9. Natural Language Processing

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

* Understand what NLP is
* Work with text data
* Identify common words and themes
* Perform simple sentiment analysis
* Interpret textual data meaningfully

---

## Why This Module Matters

Many important insights are hidden in text:

* student feedback
* survey responses
* emails and messages

Structured data tells us *what* happened.

Text tells us *why*.

---

## The Core Idea

NLP helps us extract meaning from text.

---

## Scenario

You are given student feedback comments.

Your task is to identify:

* common themes
* sentiment
* possible issues
