# Lesson: Natural Language Processing

---

## Objective

Understand how to extract meaning from text.

---

## The Common Mistake

Many learners try to:

* jump straight into models

But NLP starts with:

* cleaning
* understanding
* interpreting

---

## What is NLP?

NLP is used to:

* analyse text
* extract meaning
* identify patterns

---

## Example

Student comment:

> "The course is interesting but the workload is too heavy."

We can extract:

* positive sentiment: "interesting"
* negative sentiment: "too heavy"

---

## Key Steps

1. Clean text
2. Tokenise (split into words)
3. Count or analyse words
4. Interpret results

---

## Common Tasks

* word frequency
* sentiment analysis
* topic identification

---

## Why This Matters

Text data helps us:

* understand experiences
* identify pain points
* improve decisions

---

## Key Takeaway

NLP turns text into insight.
