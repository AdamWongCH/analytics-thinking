# Exercise: Thinking About Classification

---

## Objective

Understand classification decisions and their implications.

---

## Scenario

A model predicts whether students are at risk.

---

## Tasks

### 1. Define Outcomes

What does it mean for a student to be “at risk”?

---

### 2. Think About Errors

Which is worse:

* Missing an at-risk student?
* Flagging a student who is not at risk?

Why?

---

### 3. Decision Context

How would your answer change if:

* resources are limited?
* interventions are costly?

---

## How this connects

In this exercise, you think about classification decisions.

In the lab, you will build and evaluate a model.

---
