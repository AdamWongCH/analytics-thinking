# 7. Association

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

* Understand what association analysis is
* Identify patterns of co-occurrence
* Interpret association rules
* Apply association thinking to real-world problems

---

## Why This Module Matters

Some patterns are not about:

* prediction
* grouping

They are about:

> **things that happen together**

Association analysis helps uncover these patterns.

---

## The Core Idea

Association finds relationships such as:

> If A happens, B is likely to happen

---

## Example

Students who:

* log in frequently
* participate in forums

may also:

* submit assignments on time
