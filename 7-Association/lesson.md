# Lesson: Association Analysis

---

## Objective

Understand how to discover patterns of co-occurrence.

---

## The Common Mistake

Many learners assume:

* association implies causation

But:

* association only shows patterns

---

## What is Association?

Association analysis identifies:

* relationships between variables
* items that occur together

---

## Example

Students who:

* log in frequently
* participate in forums

may also:

* perform well

---

## Key Concepts

### Support

How often a pattern occurs

---

### Confidence

How often B happens when A happens

---

### Lift

How much more likely B is when A happens

---

## Example Rule

```text
High Logins → Assignment Submitted
```

---

## Interpretation

* High confidence → strong relationship
* High lift → meaningful association

---

## Important Reminder

Association does not imply causation.

---

## How this connects

In the exercise, you will:

* think about co-occurrence

In the lab, you will:

* generate and interpret association rules

---

## Key Takeaway

Association helps uncover hidden patterns in behaviour.
