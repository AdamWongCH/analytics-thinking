# Exercise Solutions: Association

---

## Patterns

Possible associations:

* high logins → forum participation
* forum participation → assignment submission

---

## Interpretation

Co-occurrence may suggest:

* engagement behaviours reinforce each other
* active students tend to engage across multiple channels

---

## Limitations

Association does not imply causation.

There may be hidden factors such as:

* motivation
* prior ability

---

## Note

Association helps generate hypotheses, not conclusions.
