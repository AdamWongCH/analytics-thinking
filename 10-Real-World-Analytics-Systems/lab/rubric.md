# Evaluation Rubric

---

## 1. Problem Clarity (20%)

* clearly defines the problem
* identifies key objectives

---

## 2. Data Thinking (20%)

* identifies relevant data
* understands data limitations

---

## 3. Method Selection (20%)

* chooses appropriate techniques
* explains reasoning

---

## 4. Interpretation (20%)

* extracts meaningful insights
* connects findings to context

---

## 5. Recommendations (20%)

* actionable
* realistic
* aligned with insights

---

## Overall

Focus is on:

* clarity of thinking
* logical structure
* practical usefulness
