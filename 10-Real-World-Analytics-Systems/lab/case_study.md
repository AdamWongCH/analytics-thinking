# Case Study

---

## Context

You are part of a university analytics team.

Student engagement has been declining.

---

## Task

Design an analytics approach to:

1. understand engagement
2. identify at-risk students
3. recommend actions

---

## Guiding Questions

* What patterns would you look for?
* Which techniques would you use?
* What outputs would stakeholders need?
* How would decisions be supported?

---

## Output

Write a 1–2 page response.

---

## Note

There is no single correct answer.

Focus on:

* clarity
* logic
* practical thinking
